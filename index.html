EyeGaze MOUSE.

Control mouse cursor position with your eyes under natural head movements via computer inbuilt webcam or external webcam. EyeGaze Mouse is a Webcam eye tracker software that lets you move mouse cursor using your eyes under natural head movements. You donâ€™t have to install any kind of costly retina scanning hardware or complex hardware system. All you need is EyeGaze Mouse software and a webcam. The software will track your eye gaze and move the mouse on screen accordingly. This software can be very useful for the disabled (physically disabled, motor neuron and amyotrophic lateral sclerosis patients). The EyeGaze Mouse enables patients to control the computer by blinking and moving their eyes much as an everyday computer user does. EyeGaze Mouse is very easy to setup and very easy to use. Instruction for Calibration:

  1. Sit appropriately in front of the webcam.
  2. Do proper calibration (follow the appeared point on the calibration screen with little head movement while keeping the eyes straight)
  3. Keep both eyes close at mid-point (calibration screen) until the point disappears. During this eye aspect ratio of the user is calculated which helps used in blinking.
  4. Follow the reaming point with little head movements.

Steps to Run the Software::

  • When users open the software, a new window opens with three parameters.
  • Webcam is the inbuilt laptop camera, and the USB camera is the external camera.
  • EYE_AR_CONSEC_FRAME this value indicates the number of successive frames to happen blink (users can select according to their comfort).
  • The EYE_AR_THRESH is the threshold value used to identify blink. The software automatically calculates the eye threshold value during calibration (at mid-point on calibration, users have to keep their eye closed at mid-point until the disappears mid-point).
